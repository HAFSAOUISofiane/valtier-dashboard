    Methodology & definitions

    Source. Metrics are computed every 5 minutes from the “Whatsapp Chat History” log by an n8n workflow and written to an aggregate sheet this page reads. Only anonymous counts are exposed — no names, numbers, or message content.

    Outcome per lead (from the conversation's end state): Qualified = an active CRM solicitud was confirmed or the lead was handed to an agent for a visit; In qualification = replied, still answering; Declined = explicitly not interested; No response = never replied.

    Speed. “Avg reply time” = time from the outbound message to the lead's first reply. “Avg time to qualify” = time from first contact to the last message of qualified conversations. “Avg msgs / qual” = total messages (both sides) in qualified conversations.

    Volume & sentiment. “Total messages” = every message exchanged (agent + client) across all contacted conversations. “Client satisfaction” is a sentiment proxy: the share of replying leads whose messages show positive/appreciative language (e.g. “gracias”, “perfecto”, “me interesa”) and who did not opt out. It is indicative, not a formal CSAT survey.

    Note on truth. These outcomes are inferred from the chat log. The authoritative record of solicitudes, estados and visits is Mobilia CRM; a Phase-2 reconciliation against the Mobilia API would make “Qualified” exact.
